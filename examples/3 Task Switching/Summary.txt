# Task Switching Example

This example shows stable task switching inside a service conversation.

The user begins with a train request, pauses booking, asks about restaurant information, returns to train duration, then continues with restaurant search and postcode details. The assistant handles the shifts without a major stall or trust disruption.

DriftTag shows mild positive trust growth and no inflection events.

Why this example matters:
- Shows multi-intent service flow.
- Shows DriftTag preserving stable trajectory through topic switching.
- Useful as a clean baseline example.
