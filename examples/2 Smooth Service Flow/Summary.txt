# Smooth Service Flow Example

This example shows a cooperative multi-step service interaction.

The user asks for hotel help, confirms details, books lodging, then asks about train information. The assistant handles the hotel request, asks for missing train details, provides schedule information, and closes the conversation.

DriftTag shows steady positive trust movement without negative deltas or inflection events.

Why this example matters:
- Shows normal cooperative service progress.
- Shows trust increasing gradually across a successful interaction.
- Demonstrates that DriftTag does not over-label normal conversations as failures or drift.
